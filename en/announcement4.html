We would like to invite you to try the gastronomic service.

We would like to apologize for the delay.

Scheduled/Estimated departure time at ……… .

Passengers, who are leaving the train, please remember to take your belongings and take caution.
Thank you for choosing PKP Intercity.

At the station you can change to long-distance, regional or suburban trains.
