If there is a dining area on the train, add:

We would like to invite you to try WARS’ gastronomic service in car number …… .

If the service is available only on a shortened route, add:

The gastronomic service is available until …………… station.

If there is no dining area but vending machines are available, add:

We would like to invite you to purchase drinks and snacks from the vending machines in car/cars number …… .

If there is no dining area but a mini-bar cart or vending machines are available, add:

Please be informed that there is no dining car available. We apologize for this inconvenience. Drinks and snacks may be purchased from the mini-bar cart or vending machines in car/cars number …… .

If both dining service and vending machines are available, add:

We would like to invite you to try WARS’ gastronomic service in car number …… and to purchase drinks and snacks from the vending machines in car/cars number …… .

If only a mini-bar cart is available, add:

We would like to invite you to purchase drinks and snacks from the mini-bar cart. The service will be available until …………… station.

If there is no possibility to move through the train with the mini-bar cart, add:

in car number …… .

If no gastronomic service is available, add:

Please be informed that the gastronomic service is not available. We apologize for this inconvenience.
